# Billing

The **Billing** section is found under **Credits** in the sidebar.

## How billing works

Etherly Sync charges **per active property** per month. A property is considered active (billable) for a billing period if it has at least one successful invoicing that month.

<Info>
Months with no invoiced bookings are not charged — you only pay for what you use.
</Info>

## Tiered Pricing

The price per property decreases as the number of active properties increases (graduated pricing). See current pricing under **Credits**.

## Payment

Charges are processed automatically via **Stripe** at the end of each billing period, based on the number of billable properties that month. A saved payment card is required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a card">
    **Credits** → **"Add Card"** to save a payment method.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Billing history

View detailed charges per period under **Credits** → **"History"** tab.
